 THE SCENARIO
You just got hired as a DA at FinTrack — a small fintech startup that helps SMEs (small-medium enterprises) track their business finances. Your manager Marcus hits you up Monday morning:

Marcus ⚡ 9:02 AM
"Hey, the finance team needs a quarterly expense review ASAP. The CEO is asking why we're overspending and where. We've got two data dumps — transactions and a budget plan. Can you figure out:
1. Which departments are over budget?
2. Where is most of the money actually going?
3. Are there any suspicious or anomalous transactions?
4. What's the monthly spending trend look like?
Data's in the drive. Need this by EOD Friday."
